Movement

A collective categorization of human movement.

Movement Vectors

Movement Vectors are a way of making movements comparable to each other. We create them so that we can take two different exercises and rank their similarity in terms of their impact on each muscle, richness of coordination, and connectedness to other movements.

Movement Archive

A collection all the movements humans are capable of and archive them here. While a vocabulary of movements is being established, they can be used to recommend other movements, create circuits and inspire new movements.
